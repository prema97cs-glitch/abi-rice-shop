🌾 Rice Farming Journey

From Seed to Your Family's Table

Follow the complete lifecycle of premium rice — 13 carefully managed stages from soil preparation to your doorstep.

01
Stage 1

Land Preparation

Fields are ploughed and flooded to create the perfect muddy seedbed. Wet soil is levelled carefully to ensure uniform water distribution across the entire paddy.

02
Stage 2

Seed Selection

Only the healthiest, plump seeds are chosen for cultivation. Seeds are tested for germination rate and soaked in water for 24 hours to accelerate sprouting.

03
Stage 3

Nursery Preparation

Seeds are sown densely in a small nursery plot and nurtured for 25–30 days. The seedlings develop strong root systems before being transplanted to the main field.

04
Stage 4

Transplanting

Healthy seedlings are uprooted and transplanted in rows into the flooded paddy field. Farmers carefully space each plant to allow roots to breathe and grow freely.

05
Stage 5

Irrigation

Water levels are monitored and managed throughout the growth cycle. Paddy fields are kept flooded at 5–10 cm depth to suppress weeds and maintain optimal humidity.

06
Stage 6

Natural Growth

Over 60–90 days the rice plants grow tall and lush. Natural organic fertilisers are applied at key intervals to strengthen the plants without any harmful chemicals.

07
Stage 7

Crop Protection

Natural pest management techniques are used — neem oil sprays, beneficial insects, and crop rotation. Zero harmful pesticides ensures pure, chemical-free rice for your family.

08
Stage 8

Flowering Stage

Tiny flowers emerge from each plant and are pollinated by wind. Within days golden grain heads begin to form — the most critical and beautiful stage of the rice lifecycle.

09
Stage 9

Harvesting

When grains turn golden and moisture drops to 20–25%, harvesting begins. Traditional sickle harvesting or modern combine methods are used depending on the variety.

10
Stage 10

Sun Drying

Harvested paddy is spread evenly on clean mats under direct sunlight. Natural sun drying for 3–5 days reduces moisture to the safe storage level of 12–14%.

11
Stage 11

Milling Process

Dried paddy is processed through our clean milling unit to remove husk and bran layers. Advanced sorting machines reject broken or discoloured grains for maximum purity.

12
Stage 12

Premium Packaging

Clean rice grains are weighed precisely and packed in moisture-proof bags of 1 kg, 5 kg, 10 kg and 25 kg. Every bag is sealed for freshness and quality assurance.

13
Stage 13 · Final

Ready for Your Family 🏡

Fresh, pure rice is now available at ABI Rice Shop. From our partner farms to your dining table — every grain carries the promise of quality, health, and trust since 2018.
